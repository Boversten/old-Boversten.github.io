I am currently a first-year graduate student in the Department of Aeronautics and Astronautics at MIT, advised by Prof. Chuchu Fan. I received my B.S. degree in the Department of Aerospace Engineering at UIUC. Previously, I have worked as an undergraduate research assistant with Prof. Melkior Ornik, Prof. Koki Ho, and Prof. Zachary R. Putnam.

My research interests lie at the intersection of Controls and Learning. My research goal is to develop learning-based control algorithms for autonomous systems while ensuring their safety.
